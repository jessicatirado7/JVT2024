Café 3 Razas

Graphic Design and Product Design

Project Overview

Cafe 3 Razas is a Puerto Rican owned coffee business. Cafe 3 Razas has a drawing and needed a digital logo made out of this drawing. The logo needed to represent the 3 races Puerto Ricans are made of. Spanish, Taino Indian, and African. With contributing colors to the flag and culture.

Cafe 3 Razas needed a new coffee label for better presentation for new distributions.
